Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

At Morrish M Enterprises, we aim to provide high-quality digital solutions. Because our products are digital in nature—consisting of software, educational materials, and electronic resources—we have established the following refund policy.

1. General Policy

Due to the immediate access provided upon purchase, all sales of digital goods are considered final. Once a download link has been accessed or an activation code has been issued, we cannot verify the return of the digital asset; therefore, refunds are generally not available.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

We will consider refund requests only under the following specific circumstances:

  • Technical Failure: If you are unable to access the product due to a confirmed technical issue on our server or platform.
  • Non-Delivery: If you have made a successful payment via Kora but did not receive the product or access instructions within 24 hours.
  • Duplicate Charges: In the event of a documented duplicate transaction for the same product.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a review of your purchase, please contact our support team at uzochukwusixtus15@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your Full Name
  • Transaction ID (provided by Kora at the time of payment)
  • Date of Purchase
  • A detailed description of the issue

Our team will review your claim within 3–5 business days. If approved, the refund will be processed to the original payment method used.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted if:

  • You have already accessed or downloaded the purchased digital content.
  • You have changed your mind after the purchase.
  • The issue is due to a lack of compatibility with your personal hardware or software environment (unless explicitly stated otherwise in the product description).
